WEBFISHING Wiki

WEBFISHING is a multiplayer, chatroom-based fishing game created by lamedeveloper. The game offers a cozy environment where players can catch nearly 100 different species of fish, meet new people, customize their character to their liking, play instruments, and generally be a silly little critter. Explore the WEBFISHING Wiki for comprehensive guides and information about the game.

Getting Started

How to play WEBFISHING?

When you first join the game, you'll start with a fishing rod, a handful of worms, and a dream. Through various upgrades, you'll soon know your way around the campsite like the back of your pole. Check out dedicated fishing guide on the WEBFISHING Wiki to get started!

What makes WEBFISHING special?

WEBFISHING is a multiplayer, classroom-focused fishing game created by lamedeveloper. The game offers a cozy environment where players can catch nearly 100 different species of fish, meet new people, customize their character, and generally be a silly little otter.
