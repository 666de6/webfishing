Welcome to the WEBFISHING Wiki, your comprehensive guide to the charming multiplayer fishing game created by lamedeveloper. WEBFISHING combines relaxing gameplay with social features, offering players a cozy virtual environment to fish, socialize, and collect nearly 100 different species of fish.

Development

WEBFISHING began as a passion project during Goodgis' Autumn Game Jam in 2022, where developer lamedeveloper created the initial version in just 8 days. The original release was a free-to-play single-player experience, which laid the foundation for the multiplayer version we know today.

Gameplay

WEBFISHING is a sandbox-style multiplayer game that offers various social activities centered around fishing and exploration. Players can join or host online servers to interact with others and enjoy the game's features.

Core Mechanics

  • Fishing Minigame - Hold left mouse button to reel in catches, with timed clicks for better results
  • Fishing Buddy System - Purchase an automated companion to catch fish over time
  • Diverse Catches - From various fish species to marine creatures and even trash items
  • Dynamic Sizing - Caught items can appear proportionally large compared to player avatars

Activities & Features

  • Metal Detecting - Explore areas for hidden treasures
  • Musical Expression - Play guitar with realistic string mechanics and chord systems
  • MIDI Support - Community-made mods enable MIDI file playback through in-game instruments
  • Environmental Traversal - Launch your character to reach high locations or distant areas

Progression System

  • Economy - Earn cash by selling fish and completing quests
  • Customization - Purchase avatar accessories and equipment upgrades
  • Collections - Complete fish collections and discover rare species
  • Secret Content - Find hidden items and participate in special events

Game Basics

WEBFISHING features several core game mechanics and systems that players need to understand to progress effectively.

Fishing System

Fishing forms the core gameplay loop of WEBFISHING. Through fishing, players can catch various types of fish, earn in-game currency, and purchase upgrades and equipment to enhance their fishing experience.

Fish Varieties

The game features 78 different catchable fish species:

  • 32 Freshwater fish species
  • 32 Saltwater fish species
  • 4 Special rain-exclusive fish
  • 7 Junk items
  • 2 Miscellaneous fish types

Equipment System

Bait

Consumable items purchased at the Bait Shop:

  • Start with basic worm bait
  • Unlock 5 additional bait types through progression
  • Change active bait through the Tacklebox interface

Lures

Equippable items that enhance fishing:

  • Provide various fishing bonuses
  • Bare Hook available by default
  • Additional lures unlocked through Camp Tier progression

Additional Features

Cosmetics

Purely aesthetic items that allow players to customize their appearance without affecting gameplay mechanics.

Musical Instruments

Interactive items for musical expression:

  • Guitars - Full string and chord system
  • Tambourine - Rhythmic percussion instrument
