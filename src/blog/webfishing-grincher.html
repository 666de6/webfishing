Hey fellow WEBFISHING players! There's been some discussion in our community about unusual activity in the game, particularly regarding a player known as "grincher" who has been joining various game lobbies. Let's break down what's actually happening and what you need to know.

What's Been Observed

  • Be cautious about accepting items from unknown players

Moving Forward

The development team is monitoring the situation and has advised the community to avoid drawing additional attention to these activities. The best approach is to continue enjoying the game while being mindful of basic online gaming safety practices.
