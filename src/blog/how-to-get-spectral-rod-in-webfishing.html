How To Get WEBFISHING Spectral Rod?

November 11, 2024

WEBFISHING Spectral Rod is a coveted secret fishing rod in WEBFISHING that can be obtained by collecting all five Spectral Bones scattered throughout the map. While this mysterious rod may look different from other fishing rods, it's important to note that it doesn't provide any gameplay advantages over standard rods. However, WEBFISHING Spectral Rod is a testament to a player's dedication and perseverance in the world of WEBFISHING.

How to Obtain WEBFISHING Spectral Rod?

  • Collect all five Spectral Bones:
    • Spectral Skull
    • Spectral Spine
    • Spectral Rib
    • Spectral Humerus
    • Spectral Femur
  • Visit WEBFISHING Spectral Hand behind the waterfall
  • Purchase WEBFISHING Spectral Rod for $500

WEBFISHING Spectral Hand

  • Located behind a waterfall
  • Acts as a shop where you can exchange your collected bones for WEBFISHING Spectral Rod
  • Will give you the message "you had an otherworldly bone tucked in you..." upon collecting the fifth bone
  • Appears as a mysterious figure in a hidden area

Important Notes on WEBFISHING Spectral Rod

  • WEBFISHING Spectral Rod costs $500 to purchase
  • Only cosmetic differences - no gameplay advantages
  • Requires all five bones to be collected before purchase
  • Cannot be obtained through other means
