WEBFISHING Spectral Bones Locations Guide

November 11, 2024

Hidden throughout the WEBFISHING world are five mysterious WEBFISHING Spectral Bones, each with its own unique location and character. This guide will help you track down these elusive collectibles essential for obtaining the legendary Spectral Rod.

The Five WEBFISHING Spectral Bones

There are five hidden spectral bones located around the map, each with its own distinct appearance and challenging location. Mastering the art of finding these WEBFISHING Spectral Bones is crucial for any serious WEBFISHING enthusiast.

WEBFISHING Spectral Bones Locations

  • Spectral Skull: Located east of the dock shop, on top of the ledge.
  • Spectral Rib: Located west of the dock shop, on top of the ledge.
  • Spectral Femur: Located in the north of the map, beside the river and behind a bush.
  • Spectral Humerus: Located above the Spectral Spine, at the very north-west point of the map.
  • Spectral Spine: Located North of the Lighthouse, beneath where the Spectral Humerus is located.

Collection Tips for WEBFISHING Spectral Bones

  • Use the Jump Mushrooms to reach high locations
  • Explore thoroughly around landmarks mentioned in the location descriptions
  • Each bone has a distinct appearance, making them easier to spot
  • Some bones require precise jumping and timing to reach

Once you've collected all five WEBFISHING Spectral Bones, you'll be ready to visit the How To Get Spectral Rod in Webfishing to obtain your Spectral Rod!
