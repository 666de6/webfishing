Webfishing What Was That? - Mysterious Message in WEBFISHING Explained

November 24, 2024

Hey fellow WEBFISHING players! As a veteran angler who's spent countless hours in this amazing game, I want to share something fascinating about one of the game's most intriguing mysteries - the "What Was That?" message. If you've ever seen this green text pop up in your chat and wondered what it means, you're in for a treat!

The Mystery Behind the Message

Picture this: you're peacefully fishing, enjoying the serene environment, when suddenly a mysterious green message appears saying "What Was That?" The first time I saw it, I was just as confused as you might be. But here's the exciting part - it's actually the game's way of telling you something extraordinary is about to happen!

What Does It Mean?

When you see this message, it means a Meteor has just spawned somewhere in your game world! Yes, you heard that right - a METEOR! But here's the catch - you only have 60 seconds to find it. Look up at the sky and follow the smoke trail to locate where it landed.

Pro tip: If you're serious about catching what the meteor brings, I'd recommend playing in single-player mode. While the meteor appears for everyone in multiplayer, only one player can claim the prize!

The UFO Connection

Once you find where the meteor landed (look for a brown circle in any water body - ponds, lakes, rivers, or the ocean), this is where things get really interesting. Cast your line into that brown area, and you might just catch something out of this world - literally!

The most exciting catch? The Unidentified Fish Object (UFO)! It's one of the game's most unique catches, featuring a rather peculiar dog-faced appearance. Trust me, it's one of those catches that will make you do a double-take!

Tips for Success

  • Stay alert for the green "What Was That?" message
  • Immediately look to the sky for smoke trails
  • Act fast - you only have 60 seconds!
  • Look for brown circles in any water body
  • Consider playing in single-player mode for better chances

This mysterious message is just one of the many quirky and fun elements that make WEBFISHING such a special game. It adds an element of excitement and unpredictability to what might otherwise be a calm fishing experience. Every time I see that green text now, I get a rush of excitement knowing there's a chance to add another UFO to my collection!
